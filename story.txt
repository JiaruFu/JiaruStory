He loved to watch his couple neighbors having fun in their gardens and with their children. Sometimes, when they came back from a long family trip, they would bring little gifts to him. But overrall, compared to other people, Martin was the weirdest guy ever. He loved how he lived until one day a mysterious stranger camed to this twon and totally changed his life..
